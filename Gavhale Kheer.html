Sweetmeat Making-Gavhale Kheer

Although the process might be difficult, this 600 year old dish is bound to bring a smile to your face. Gavhale is essentially handmade dough balls that is made from scratch and cooked into this delicious kheer.Offered to goddess Gauri as naivedya on Ganpati, this one is a fading tradition in the modern times.However it also made on other auspicious and festive occasions. It also promises health benefits. So when in Maharashtra, do try this milk based relish out!
